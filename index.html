About

About

Hi! My name is Matteo. I'm a bass player, composer and producer based in Amsterdam and Brussels.

I take part in all kinds of creative projects across the world, from instrumental techno to indie rock, I love it all. Besides co-leading boundary-pushing group Neon River, I have recorded and performed with Oracle Sisters, Jean-Paul Groove, Jozef Dumoulin, Mark Schilders, Melissa Aldana, Deine Freunde, Chaerin Im, Antoine Pierre, PEET, Bart Wirtz, Guillaume Vierset, Jasper Blom…

I'm available for touring, studio recordings (in person or remote), and teaching.
